The Real Story

"Behind every polished professional facade is a human being who has stumbled, failed, learned, and grown. This is that story - unfiltered, imperfect, and real."

The Messy Timeline

2010-2015

The "Lost Years"

Dropped out of college twice. Worked dead-end jobs. Lived in my parents' basement at 24. Everyone else seemed to have it figured out while I was still asking "What do I want to be when I grow up?"

💔 Reality Check: Success isn't linear, and comparison is the thief of joy.
2016-2018

Rock Bottom & Rebuilding

Got fired from a job I hated (best thing that ever happened). Decided to go back to school at 26. Worked nights, studied days, survived on ramen and stubbornness. Failed my first law school entrance exam. Took it again.

⚡ Lesson: Sometimes you need to lose everything to find what really matters.
2019-2021

The Grind Years

Law school while working full-time. 4 hours of sleep became normal. Imposter syndrome hit hard - surrounded by people who seemed naturally brilliant while I was just trying to keep up. Almost quit three times.

🎯 Truth: Persistence beats talent when talent doesn't persist.
2022-Present

Finding My Voice

Started to see that my "unconventional" path was actually my strength. The failures taught me resilience. The late start gave me perspective. The struggles made me human. Still figuring it out, but now I'm okay with that.

🌱 Growth: Your mess becomes your message.
